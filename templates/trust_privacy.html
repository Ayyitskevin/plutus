Privacy policy

Last updated June 2026 · Draft for photographer review

Short version: Client galleries belong to the photographer. We use images only to recommend print bundles and fulfill orders. We do not train models on your clients' photos. Each tenant sees only their own galleries and orders.

Who we are

Plutus is a print and album upsell layer for professional photographers. This policy covers photographers using the SaaS dashboard, client offer pages, and checkout.

What we collect

  • Tenant account — studio name, notify email, API keys (hashed), billing status.
  • Galleries — images uploaded or synced from Mise, plus vision metadata from Argus.
  • Client checkout — name and email when a client pays (for receipts and fulfillment).
  • Orders — line items, totals, lab status, and audit events for support.

How we use data

  • Generate print bundle recommendations and storefront offer links.
  • Process Stripe payments and hand orders to the configured print lab.
  • Send transactional email (order alerts, client confirmations) when SMTP is configured.

What we do not do

  • We do not train foundation models on your clients' gallery images.
  • We do not sell gallery data to third parties.
  • We do not expose one tenant's galleries to another tenant.

Retention & deletion

Galleries and orders are retained while your account is active. Contact your studio operator or Plutus admin to request tenant deletion.

Questions

Privacy questions: use the notify email on your studio account or contact the operator who invited you to Plutus.
