The service

Plutus helps photographers turn delivered galleries into print and album bundles, share client offer links, and collect payment through Stripe. Lab fulfillment is provided via configured adapters (mock, WHCC stub, or live lab when wired).

Your responsibilities

  • You are responsible for client communication, refunds, and studio policies beyond checkout.
  • Keep API keys confidential — they grant full access to your tenant data.

Client checkout

Clients purchasing through your offer links enter a transaction with your studio. Plutus provides the technical checkout and order handoff; pricing and fulfillment policies are yours.

Billing

SaaS subscription terms, trial limits, and refunds are governed by your Stripe plan and separate billing agreement when enabled.
